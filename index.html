Dragon Blooded

Überarbeitete Homebrew-Lineage mit Spellcaster-Fokus. Diese Version ersetzt klassische Farb-Ancestries durch euer Main- und Sub-Element-System und bindet zusätzlich eure Hybrid-Dragons direkt in dieselbe Seite ein.

Lineage Concept

Dragon Blooded sind keine normalen Dragonborn. Ihr Blut speichert drakonische Arkana, die sich durch Breath Weapon, Element-Resistenz, Sorcerer-artige Magie, temporäre Flügel und später eine echte drakonische Form entfalten.

Caster-Oriented Sorcerer Synergy Main & Sub Elements Hybrid Dragons True Dragon Form at Level 8

Empfohlener Build

  • Class: Sorcerer
  • Stats: +2 Charisma, +2 Constitution
  • Element-Fokus: Wähle das Element, zu dem deine Damage-Spells passen
  • Starke Gifts: Scaled Focus, Wing Burst, Elemental Surge

Was hier geändert wurde

  • Normale Farb-Ancestries wurden entfernt.
  • Stattdessen nutzt die Rasse Main- und Sub-Elemente aus eurem Drachensystem.
  • Hybride sind als separate Drachenformen mit Rollen und Beschreibungen eingebaut.

Dragon Blooded Traits

Creature Type
You are a Humanoid.
Size
You are Medium.
Speed
Your walking speed is 30 feet.
Ability Score Increase
Your Charisma score increases by 2, and your Constitution score increases by 2.
Elemental Ancestry
Choose one Main Element and one Sub-Element from that element group. This determines the damage theme, resistance, and flavor of your racial features.
Elemental Resistance
You have resistance to the primary damage type associated with your chosen element path.
Breath Weapon
When you take the Attack action on your turn, you can replace one of your attacks with an exhalation of destructive draconic energy. If you do not have Extra Attack, you may use it as an action. Each creature in the area makes a saving throw against DC = 8 + proficiency bonus + Charisma modifier. On a failure, a creature takes 1d10 ancestry damage, or half as much on a success. The damage becomes 2d10 at level 5, 3d10 at level 11, and 4d10 at level 17. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus per long rest.
Arcane Bloodline
You know Prestidigitation. You also know one additional cantrip of your choice from the Sorcerer spell list. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for these spells.
Draconic Gifts
At level 3 and again at level 5, you gain one Draconic Gift of your choice from the Dragon Blooded gift list.
True Dragon Form
Starting at level 8, you can use your action to awaken the full might of your draconic blood and transform into a true dragon-like form. This transformation lasts until you end it as a bonus action, until you are incapacitated, or until you die. While in this form, you gain a 40-foot flying speed, your AC becomes 13 + Dexterity modifier if higher than your current AC, you may use your Breath Weapon as a bonus action, once per turn you may add your Charisma modifier to one damage roll of a spell that deals your ancestry's damage type, and you have advantage on Charisma (Intimidation) checks. Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest.
Gift Option

Wing Burst

As a bonus action, you manifest spectral draconic wings for 1 minute. For the duration, you gain a flying speed equal to your walking speed. Once used, this gift refreshes on a long rest.

Gift Option

Scaled Focus

When you cast a spell that deals damage matching your Elemental Ancestry, you can reroll one damage die of that spell. You can do this a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus per long rest.

Gift Option

Draconic Mind

You can communicate telepathically with any creature you can see within 30 feet, provided it understands at least one language. You also have advantage on saving throws against being charmed.

Gift Option

Elemental Surge

Once on each of your turns, when you deal damage with a spell that matches your Elemental Ancestry, you can add your proficiency bonus to one damage roll of that spell.

Gift Option

Mantle of Scales

When you take damage of the type associated with your Elemental Ancestry, you can use your reaction to harden your scales and gain temporary hit points equal to your Charisma modifier + your proficiency bonus. Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.

Build Advice

Beste Caster-Picks

Level 3: Scaled Focus. Level 5: Wing Burst für Mobilität oder Elemental Surge für mehr Output. Draconic Mind ist die sichere Utility-Option.
