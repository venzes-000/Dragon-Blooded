Dragon Blooded

A homebrew dragon lineage built for a spellcaster-heavy playstyle. This version keeps the strong draconic flavor, pushes naturally toward Sorcerer, and spreads the power spikes across levels instead of frontloading everything at level 1.

Lineage Concept

Dragon Blooded are not just descendants of dragons. Their blood actively stores draconic arcana, awakening through breath, elemental resistance, magical instinct, spectral wings, and finally a permanent battle-form transformation.

Caster-Oriented Sorcerer Synergy Breath Weapon at Level 1 Flight Access True Dragon Form at Level 8

Recommended Build

  • Class: Sorcerer
  • Stats: +2 Charisma, +2 Constitution
  • Best early Gift: Scaled Focus
  • Best later Gift: Wing Burst or Elemental Surge

Balance Direction

  • No overloaded level 1 package beyond what matters.
  • Flight is controlled before the full transformation.
  • True Dragon Form is strong, but still not a monster stat block.

Dragon Blooded Traits

Creature Type
You are a Humanoid.
Size
You are Medium.
Speed
Your walking speed is 30 feet.
Ability Score Increase
Your Charisma score increases by 2, and your Constitution score increases by 2.
Draconic Ancestry
Choose a dragon ancestry. Your ancestry determines the damage type used by your racial features.
Draconic Resistance
You have resistance to the damage type associated with your Draconic Ancestry.
Breath Weapon
When you take the Attack action on your turn, you can replace one of your attacks with an exhalation of destructive draconic energy. If you do not have Extra Attack, you may use it as an action. Each creature in the area makes a saving throw against DC = 8 + proficiency bonus + Charisma modifier. On a failure, a creature takes 1d10 ancestry damage, or half as much on a success. The damage becomes 2d10 at level 5, 3d10 at level 11, and 4d10 at level 17. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus per long rest.
Arcane Bloodline
You know Prestidigitation. You also know one additional cantrip of your choice from the Sorcerer spell list. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for these spells.
Draconic Gifts
At level 3 and again at level 5, you gain one Draconic Gift of your choice from the Dragon Blooded gift list.
True Dragon Form
Starting at level 8, you can use your action to awaken the full might of your draconic blood and transform into a true dragon-like form. This transformation lasts until you end it as a bonus action, until you are incapacitated, or until you die. While in this form, you gain a 40-foot flying speed, your AC becomes 13 + Dexterity modifier if higher than your current AC, you may use your Breath Weapon as a bonus action, once per turn you may add your Charisma modifier to one damage roll of a spell that deals your ancestry's damage type, and you have advantage on Charisma (Intimidation) checks. Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest.
Gift Option

Wing Burst

As a bonus action, you manifest spectral draconic wings for 1 minute. For the duration, you gain a flying speed equal to your walking speed. Once used, this gift refreshes on a long rest.

Gift Option

Scaled Focus

When you cast a spell that deals damage matching your Draconic Ancestry, you can reroll one damage die of that spell. You can do this a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus per long rest.

Gift Option

Draconic Mind

You can communicate telepathically with any creature you can see within 30 feet, provided it understands at least one language. You also have advantage on saving throws against being charmed.

Gift Option

Elemental Surge

Once on each of your turns, when you deal damage with a spell that matches your Draconic Ancestry, you can add your proficiency bonus to one damage roll of that spell.

Gift Option

Mantle of Scales

When you take damage of the type associated with your Draconic Ancestry, you can use your reaction to harden your scales and gain temporary hit points equal to your Charisma modifier + your proficiency bonus. Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.

Build Advice

Best Sorcerer Picks

Level 3: Scaled Focus is the cleanest early damage value. Level 5: Wing Burst for mobility or Elemental Surge for raw output. Draconic Mind is the safest utility pick.

Level Progression

1

Base Identity

You start with the parts that actually define the race.
  • Draconic Ancestry
  • Damage Resistance
  • Breath Weapon
  • Prestidigitation + 1 Sorcerer cantrip
3

First Draconic Gift

This is where you start specializing into flight, spell damage, mind power, or defense.
5

Second Draconic Gift

Your breath weapon scales to 2d10 and the race begins to feel distinctly stronger.
8

True Dragon Form

Main action to activate. Stays active until you end it, get incapacitated, or die.
  • 40-foot flying speed
  • AC floor of 13 + Dex
  • Breath Weapon becomes bonus action
  • Add Charisma mod once per turn to matching spell damage
11

Breath Upgrade

Breath Weapon damage becomes 3d10.
17

Late-Game Scale

Breath Weapon damage becomes 4d10, keeping the ancestry feature relevant in high-level play.
